This app shows how your annual property tax bill is allocated across Arcadia’s budget categories. Percentages are normalized to 100% if needed so the totals match your bill to the penny.

Education
Local share of K-12 education funding and related services.
Public Safety
Policing and by-law enforcement, including community safety programs.
Infrastructure
Roads, bridges, culverts, water/wastewater systems, and major maintenance.
Fire Services
Fire prevention, response, equipment, and station operations.
Transportation
Public transit, traffic operations, signage, and snow/ice control.
Parks
Parks, trails, recreation facilities, programming, and maintenance.
Social Services
Housing supports, community grants, and social programming.
Administration
General government, finance, HR, IT, customer service.
Debt Services
Interest and principal repayments on municipal borrowing.
Environmental
Waste management, recycling, environmental monitoring and programs.
Health
Public health contributions and local health initiatives.
Emergency
Emergency planning, EOC operations, and disaster response contingencies.

Tip: In Compare mode, the top differences are marked with a small “diff” tag beside the category.
