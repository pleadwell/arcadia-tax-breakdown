This app shows how your annual property tax bill is allocated across Arcadia’s budget categories which are the official categories of the Government of New Brunswicks (GNB). Percentages are normalized to 100% if needed so the totals match your bill to the penny.

General Government Services
Office staff, councillors, office building, admin costs, etc
Fire Protection
Fire prevention, response, equipment, and station operations.
Police Protection
RCMP contract through Province.
Environmental Health Services
Solid Waste (Garbage pickup).
Transportation Services
Local roads, DTI costs for summer/winter maintenance, street lighting.
Fiscal Services
Interest and principal repayments on municipal borrowing (Fire Protection/Equipment & Municipal roads)
Recreation & Cultural Services
Recreation, community grants, and social programming.
Public Health Services
Medical clinics (Cambridge-Narrows & Gagetown)
Emergency Measures
Emergency measures.
Other Protection
Mainly SPCA animal control.
Other Services
N/A.

Tip: In Compare mode, the top differences are marked with a small “diff” tag beside the category.
